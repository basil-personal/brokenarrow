About Us

Morningside Capital specializes in acquiring and revitalizing software companies with strong foundations. We seek out businesses with loyal customer bases and low churn rates, even if their growth has plateaued. Our unique approach creates value for both entrepreneurs and their established businesses.

Our Mission

To provide a win-win solution for software entrepreneurs and their loyal customers by injecting capital, expertise, and fresh perspectives into great businesses.

What We Do

  1. Acquire: We purchase software businesses with solid customer bases and proven track records.
  2. Empower: We provide entrepreneurs with the capital they need to pursue their next ventures.
  3. Invest: We infuse acquired businesses with capital and expertise to drive their next phase of growth.
  4. Collaborate: We offer entrepreneurs the option to remain invested in their original businesses, fostering continued partnership.
