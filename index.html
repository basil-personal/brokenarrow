Morningside Capital

About Us

Morningside Capital specializes in acquiring software companies with strong foundations and unlocking their next stage of growth. We focus on businesses built by tech-first founders who are ready to take their sales and marketing to the next level. Our unique approach creates value for both entrepreneurs and their established businesses.

Our Mission

To drive step-function growth in great software businesses by combining capital, expertise, and fresh perspectives, ultimately creating outstanding outcomes for founders, employees, and customers.

What We Do

  1. Identify: We seek out software businesses with untapped potential for rapid growth.
  2. Invest: We provide the capital and resources needed to unlock new levels of success.
  3. Accelerate: Our team works closely with founders and employees to drive transformative growth.
  4. Collaborate: We offer founders the opportunity to stay involved and benefit from their company's success.
