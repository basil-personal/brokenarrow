About Us

At Morningside Capital, we have a unique approach to investing in software companies. We seek out businesses with strong foundations, loyal customer bases, and low churn rates, even if their growth has plateaued. Our team of experienced software industry veterans brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to each acquisition, allowing us to inject new life into these companies and unlock their full potential.

We believe in creating value for all stakeholders - entrepreneurs, customers, and employees. By providing the capital, resources, and strategic guidance needed to revitalize these businesses, we aim to create a win-win situation for everyone involved.

Our Mission
